💎 Caratlane Sales Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Caratlane’s sales performance, a TATA product, through interactive visualizations and KPIs.
It highlights business insights such as total sales, invoice counts, discounts, and product category trends — enabling data-driven decision-making.

📊 Key Insights
Total Sales: ₹4M

Invoices: 108

Made-to-Order (MTO): 29

Return to Store (RTS): 79

Total Discount: ₹710K

🧩 Visuals Included
Sales by State: Karnataka leads with 86.42% of total sales.

Sales by Salesperson: Farhan and Meher contribute major portions of revenue.

Sales by Category: Earrings and Rings are top-performing products.

Daily Sales Trend: Steady growth with a sharp rise toward the end of the period.
